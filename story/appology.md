# The Apology — Context

## What Happened

During a fight where we were both mad and cursing, I said something cruel to Dounia — I told her she is not worthy of marriage, halal, and respect.

I should have never said that. It was not my intention to hurt her. I was angry in the moment, but that does not excuse it.

---

## Why I Was Angry (Not a Justification)

I have been trying to marry her for more than a year and she keeps delaying. That frustration had been building.

Part of why marriage matters so much to me is that she takes cabs daily to work in Casablanca, which is dangerous, and I have been wanting to be able to pick her up as her husband — to protect her. That helplessness was eating at me.

---

## What I Actually Feel

I deeply regret what I said. It is the opposite of what I believe about her.

She is someone who deserves every form of respect, protection, and love. The whole reason I have been pushing for marriage is because I want to give her exactly that — halal, honoring, real.

---

## Purpose of This File

This is reference material for the apology experience this project is now becoming. The words I said were the wound. This project is the attempt to heal it — to show her who she really is through my eyes, and to make clear that what I said in anger was a lie I never believed.
